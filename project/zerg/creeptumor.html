Information: Once the first Creep Tumor has been put down on creep, that Creep Tumor may generate another to become the active tumor. Only 1 tumor from a series can be the active one, and it is always the last one spawned.

It takes 15 seconds for a Creep Tumor to gestate, and while doing so it is vulnerable to attacks. Once it has finished gestating, it burrows itself and becomes invisible to enemies without detection.

Each Creep Tumor spreads creep out to 10 game units from itself, but once killed the creep will recede unless other creep spawning units/buildings cover that area.

Upon finishing, a Creep Tumor must wait 15 seconds before it can spread another Creep Tumor.

+1 Weapons vs Zerglings

When Zealots have a +1 weapon advantage versus Zerglings, they will kill Zerglings in two attacks instead of three. This makes Zealots very effecient against Zerglings until the zerg catches up in armor upgrades.

Because of this, there are a handful of timing attack that take advantage of the effecient +1 weapon zealots, forcing the zerg to make spines, roaches, or some other unit than zerglings unless the zerg wants to trade inefficiently.

  • Overview


The creep tumor is a structure which extends the creep. It resembles a cluster of boils protruding from the creep. Creep tumors are created by the new Queens or by other Creep Tumors and must be built on creep.

Creep is a living zerg organism. It has a purple coloration and feels thick and slimy. Creep is psionically-sensitive and may be influenced and controlled by psionic entities. It is used to nourish zerg structures and larvae.
