Unit Information
Hellion: Fast scout. Has a flame attack that damages all enemy units in its line of fire. Can attack ground units.

Siege Tank: Heavy tank. Can be upgraded to switch into Siege Mode to provide long-range artillery support. Can attack ground units.

Thor: Heavy assualt mech. Capable of jumping up and down cliffs. Can attack ground and air units.

Add-On Information
Reactor: The Reactor on a Factory allows it to create two Hellions at a time.

Tech Lab: The Tech Lab on a Factory allows it to create Siege Tanks and Thors (with an Armory). Information: Most Terran buildings are allowed to lift off and relocate. This is particularly useful for swapping add-ons between Terran Production Facilities. It takes a building two seconds to lift off or land.

Once a building has been lifted off, it becomes a flying unit and is able to move at a speed of 0.9375.

In order for a building to land back down, it requires nothing to be below it when attempting to land. Burrowed units and creep will stop a building from being able to land as well.

+1 Weapons vs Zerglings

When Zealots have a +1 weapon advantage versus Zerglings, they will kill Zerglings in two attacks instead of three. This makes Zealots very effecient against Zerglings until the zerg catches up in armor upgrades.

Because of this, there are a handful of timing attack that take advantage of the effecient +1 weapon zealots, forcing the zerg to make spines, roaches, or some other unit than zerglings unless the zerg wants to trade inefficiently.

  • Overview


A factory is an automated terran structure which acts as the primary production center for vehicles, structures, and ammunition. The Factory also houses the materials required for a Nuclear Missle to be created at the Ghost Academy.

After the Brood Wars, the Terrans replaced the Machine Shop with the Tech Lab. This gave the Terrans much more versatility to adapt their buildings for the need required at a certain time. The use of one add-on between multiple structures also reduces resources needed, as the Terrans could now use the same Tech Lab on multiple buildings instead of creating a seperate add-on for each.

Connecting a Tech Lab to a factory gives it the nessecary schematics for Siege Tanks, Goliaths, and Thors. The Tech Lab also has schematics for various upgrades like the Infernal Pre-Igniter for Hellions or Charon Boosters for Goliaths. Connecting a reactor allows the Factory to simultaneously produce more simple vehicles like the Vulture or Hellion.
